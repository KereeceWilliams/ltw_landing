Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I still donate if I don’t plan on participating in the hike?

- Yes, you can sign up as a “virtual hiker”. Even though you don’t participate in the hike, you will still receive a t-shirt.

2. If I am a vendor and have just applied to sell my goods at the hike, how long do I have to wait to hear back if my application has been accepted or rejected?

-If you apply to be a vendor, you should be hearing back from us in 1-2 weeks with an official decision.

3. If I am a team leader, do I need to register my teammates?

-If you are a team leader and have created a team, you will need your teammates to register on our website, and then you will need to manually add their names to your team.

4. If I sign up this year, will I need to sign up again next year, or will my information be saved?

-If you sign up this year, you will not need to sign up again next year, but you will need to rejoin a team, as that particular bit of information will not be saved.

If you have any further questions please ask us though our contact form below
